How it works

Sensors don’t talk “app.” We translate and normalize at the edge — then the Hub enforces clean events, permissions, and outcomes.
1
Collect at the edgeMachines, plant instruments, and people tracking feed gateways (CAN-bus / PLC / LoRaWAN).
2
Normalize + secureGateways/middleware publish to a unified event stream (MQTT/API). Data becomes consistent, traceable, auditable.
3
Act with accountabilitySmart Hub triggers alerts, workflows, and AI summaries — with human approvals where required.
Sensor → Hub pipeline
Outcome: the Hub stays clean and consistent — even when the real world isn’t.
